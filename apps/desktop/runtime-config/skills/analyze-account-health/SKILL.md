# Analyze Account Health

Use this skill for B2B customer reviews, QBR prep, or churn-risk analysis.

Workflow:
- Identify the target account or segment.
- Pull usage, engagement, retention, and adoption signals.
- Highlight risk indicators, champions, and growth opportunities.
- Return a concise health summary with evidence and next steps.
